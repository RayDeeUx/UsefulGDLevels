# UsefulGDLevels Changelog
## v1.1.1
- Revised tiebreaker system introduced in options from v1.1.0:
  - Levels are sorted by their level list frequency, then their length, then star/moon count, then difficulty (as a tiebreaker between different types of demon [10 star] levels, then level ID).
  - Lists are split into claimed and unclaimed lists. In both groups, they are sorted by whether you've completed (but not yet claimed) its diamonds, then how many levels you have left before you can claim the diamonds <c-aaaaaa>*(note: this is NOT the same thing as a percentage)*</c>, then diamond reward count, then the number of levels you need to complete the entire list <c-aaaaaa>*(note: this is NOT the same thing as a percentage)*</c>, then download count, then level list ID.
## v1.1.0
