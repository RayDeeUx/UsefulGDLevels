# UsefulGDLevels
https://gdcolon.com/usefulgdlevels integration inside Geometry Dash.

You <c-ff0000>***__ARE REQUIRED TO__***</c> use this mod along with [BetterInfo](mod:cvolton.betterinfo) for more precise level list completion tracking.

<mod:cvolton.betterinfo>

Indicates the number of level lists a level is in (if it's four level lists or more) and your progress on each, as well as navigation shortcuts to these levels and level lists.

Made with permission from Colon after a mod suggestion from [Parallex](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCl6Jcm-ePOArJ-NFiHb6cWg).
